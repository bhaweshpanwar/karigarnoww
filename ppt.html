03
The Problem
🏗️

Idle Workforce

Over 50M workers wait 3-5 hours daily at street corners (Chourahas) with zero visibility.

🔍

Discovery Gap

Homeowners spend hours finding workers with no guarantee of skill or fair pricing.

🛡️

Trust Gap

No identity verification. Strangers enter homes with zero background checks or accountability.

04
The Solution

The Thekedar-Led Model.

  • Hybrid Management: Contractors manage teams of 5-50 workers.
  • Smartphone Independence: Individual workers don't need phones; the Thekedar handles digital coordination.
  • Escrow Security: Payments are locked until the customer is satisfied.
